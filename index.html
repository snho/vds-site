About Us

Vanguard Defence Systems is a military focused research and development company. Our aim is to evolve the skillsets of the modern soldier, by implementing cutting edge innovation into the daily life of warfighter.

Our Mission

The mission of Vanguard Defence Systems is to develop the most advanced training systems, tools and services for the Australian Defence Force in order to equip our soldiers with the skills and knowledge to survive in high threat environments.

Projects

ARES - The Advance Reactive Engagement System (ARES) aims to relieve this monotony in training and motivate the participation, engagement, and feedback provided in marksmanship training through providing a user customisable reactive smart-target system.
